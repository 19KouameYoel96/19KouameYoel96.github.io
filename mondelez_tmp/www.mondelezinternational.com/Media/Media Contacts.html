FOR JOURNALISTS IN NORTH AMERICAN TIME ZONES

Please call our Media Helpline at +1-847-943-5678.

Please note that the media helpline phone number is exclusively for journalists. Only calls from news media will be returned.

The Media Helpline is staffed during regular business hours: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m., Monday - Friday, Chicago Time. Inquiries received by the Helpline outside of normal business hours will be addressed the next business day.
